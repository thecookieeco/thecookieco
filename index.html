About Our Cookies

Made from premium quality ingredients, each vanilla bean sugar cookie is topped with vanilla fondant icing and stamped with your own personal message. Catering for all occasions – Birthdays, Baby Showers, Hens Parties, Weddings plus more, our cookies come in a range of shapes and are approx 65mm in diameter, complimentary wrapped in premium cello packaging & heat sealed for freshness.
